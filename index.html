Privacy Policy

How NoteTaker handles your data, ads, and device information.
Effective Date: July 2026 Developer: Darrell (Cozique)

Overview

NoteTaker is a simple note‑taking application designed to let users create, edit, and manage personal notes on their Android device. This privacy policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and how it is protected.

Information We Collect

NoteTaker itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All notes you create remain on your device only.

However, the app uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect certain data automatically, including:

  • Device identifiers
  • Approximate location
  • Ad interaction and performance data
  • Crash or diagnostic information

This collection is handled by Google, not by NoteTaker. You can learn more about Google’s data practices here: https://policies.google.com/privacy .

How Your Data Is Used

App Data: NoteTaker does not send your notes or personal information to any server. All note content stays local on your device unless you remove the app or clear its data.

AdMob Data: Google may use collected data to:

  • Serve personalized or non‑personalized ads
  • Improve ad relevance and performance
  • Detect fraud, abuse, or invalid activity

You can control ad personalization in your Google account settings or device ad preferences.

Data Sharing

NoteTaker does not share any data with third parties. AdMob (Google) may share data according to its own privacy policy. NoteTaker does not have direct access to this data and does not store it.

Security

Because NoteTaker does not collect or store personal data on external servers, the risk of your notes being transmitted or leaked is minimized. Your notes remain on your device unless you delete the app or clear its data.

Children’s Privacy

NoteTaker is not directed toward children under the age of 13. AdMob may serve general‑audience ads, but NoteTaker does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated occasionally to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. Updates will be published on this page, and the effective date will be revised.
