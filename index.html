⭐ Privacy Policy for NoteTaker Effective Date: July 2026 Developer: bank21khz 📘 Overview NoteTaker is a simple note‑taking application designed to let users create, edit, and manage personal notes on their Android device. This privacy policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and how it is protected. 🔒 Information We Collect NoteTaker itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All notes you create remain on your device only. However, the app uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect certain data automatically. This includes: Device identifiers Approximate location Ad interaction data Crash or performance data This collection is handled by Google, not by NoteTaker. You can learn more about Google’s data practices here: https://policies.google.com/privacy 📡 How Your Data Is Used App Data NoteTaker does not send your notes or personal information to any server. All note content stays local on your device. AdMob Data Google may use collected data to: Serve personalized or non‑personalized ads Improve ad performance Detect fraud and abuse You can control ad personalization in your Google account settings. 🔁 Data Sharing NoteTaker does not share any data with third parties. AdMob (Google) may share data according to its own privacy policy. NoteTaker does not have access to this data. 🛡️ Security Because NoteTaker does not collect or store personal data, there is no risk of your notes being transmitted or leaked. Your notes remain on your device unless you delete the app or clear its data. 👶 Children’s Privacy NoteTaker is not directed toward children under 13. AdMob may serve general audience ads, but NoteTaker does not collect any information from children. 🔄 Changes to This Policy This privacy policy may be updated occasionally.
